Giving for Pets is an organization that helps to provide pet supplies to those who have fallen on hard times.
This can include people who have experienced homelessness, lost jobs, and are having to choose between
providing for themselves or for their animals.
We are here to help lessen the burden so they may keep their pets instead of taking them to an animal shelter.
